"4030-system" (4030 Call-System) is an [[Iran]]ian phone-system which has been made since the first days of COVID-19 outbreak in [[Iran]] for expert consulting and corona-screening (of coronavirus suspects). This call-system has been launched by the [[Execution of Imam Khomeini's Order]] (Barakat Tel) and in cooperation with the [[Ministry of Health and Medical Education]] and [[Telecommunication Company of Iran|Iran Telecommunication]] in order to screen Iranian families at the field of Coronavirus disease 2019. There are 2000 phone lines and 2,200 doctors/paramedics across the country who are servicing at/by this call system.
