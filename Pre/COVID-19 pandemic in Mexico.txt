Title: COVID-19 pandemic in Mexico
ID: 982172890
FULLTEXT: <td class="diff-addedline"><div>The '''COVID-19 pandemic in Mexico''' is part of the ongoing [[COVID-19 pandemic|worldwide pandemic]] of [[coronavirus disease 2019]] ({{nowrap|COVID-19}}) caused by [[severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus&amp;nbsp;2]] ({{nowrap|SARS-CoV-2}}). The virus was confirmed to have reached [[Mexico]] in February 2020. However, the [[Consejo Nacional de Ciencia y Tecnología (Mexico)|National Council of Science and Technology]] (CONACYT) reported two cases of [[Coronavirus disease 2019|COVID-19]] in mid-January 2020 in the [[List of states of Mexico|states]] of [[Nayarit]] and [[Tabasco]], one case per state.&lt;ref&gt;{{cite web |url=https://datos.covid-19.conacyt.mx/#COMNac |title=Covid-19 CONACYT |date= April 21, 2020 |language=es}}&lt;/ref&gt; As of October 4, there had been 761,665 confirmed cases of COVID-19 in Mexico and 79,088 reported deaths,&lt;ref&gt;{{cite news |url=https://coronavirus.gob.mx/datos/|title= Información General|author=Government of Mexico |date= May 26, 2020 |language=es |website=Gobierno de México<ins class="diffchange diffchange-inline">}}&lt;/ref&gt;&lt;ref&gt;{{cite news |url= https://www.elfinanciero.com.mx/nacional/mexico-rompe-record-de-muertes-diarias-registradas-de-covid-19-con-2-789-y-de-casos-confirmados-con-28-115 |title= Muertes por COVID-19 se disparan a 81,877 y los casos a 789,780 tras cambio en metodología |author= Redacción |date= October 5, 2020 |language=es |website= El Financiero</ins>}}&lt;/ref&gt; although the [[Secretariat of Health (Mexico)|Secretariat of Health]], through the ''"Programa Centinela"'' (Spanish for "Sentinel Program") estimated in mid July 2020 that there were more than 2,875,734 cases in Mexico, because they were considering the total number of cases confirmed as a [[Sample (statistics)|statistical sample]].&lt;ref&gt;{{cite web|url= http://iigea.com/amag/covid-19/ |title= COVID-19 Seguimiento México |language=es | website= IIGEA |accessdate=July 13, 2020}}&lt;/ref&gt;</div></td>
